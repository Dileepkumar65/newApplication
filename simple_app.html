Welcome to the CS Learning Guide

Your comprehensive guide to mastering computer science from first principles

Select a topic from the sidebar to begin learning.

This guide takes a unique "build from scratch" approach that teaches you how to create computational tools from first principles, without relying on high-level abstractions.

By the end, you'll understand computers from the transistor level all the way up to complex applications.
